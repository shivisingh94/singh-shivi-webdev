  • Rex Tillerson confirmed as secretary of state

  • The Senate confirmed Rex Tillerson to be secretary of state by a 56-43 vote. The former Exxon Mobil chief executive was criticized by Democrats and Republicans because of his alleged closed ties to top Russian officials, including President Vladimir Putin. But in the end, all Republicans voted for his confirmation as did four members of the Democratic caucus.
